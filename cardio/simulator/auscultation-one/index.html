Welcome to the Cardiac Confidence Lab

6 textbook cardiac cases — Think of this as brain training of your cardiac short case: sort out your top diagnosis, argue through your differentials, and assess severity. Real patients are more subtle and may have more than one murmur — but mastering the archetypes here gives you the framework to tackle those more subtle scenarios and discuss your findings confidently

  • Click Start listening, then click through the auscultation spots to find where the murmur is loudest and where it radiates — then explore manoeuvres, positions, and stethoscope pieces.
  • The carotid pulse light flashes during systole — use it to time the murmur.
  • Click on the examination findings around the patient to unveil other important clues for diagnosis and severity.
  • Hit Face the Examiners when you're ready to present.

Have fun!
