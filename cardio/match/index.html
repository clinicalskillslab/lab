How to Play

  1. Review the murmurs in the answer bank on the right.
  2. Drag (or tap) each murmur tile onto the correct category.
  3. Not all murmurs listed are systolic — leave distractors in the bank.
  4. Tap a placed tile to return it to the bank.
  5. Press Check Answers when ready.
Tip Each category shows a phonocardiogram with S1 and S2 landmarks. Use the shape of the murmur to guide you.
